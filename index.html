Have you heard that Nepalese food isn’t so great? I have. Luckily I’ve been to Nepal 3 times, so I can tell you that it’s not true. Back when Chef and I trekked the Annapurna Circuit we enjoyed discovering Nepalese food and on our second trip, trekking the Everest Region with kids, we were eating new regional variations on local Nepal food. Sure, Dal Baht can become a bit monotonous on the trekking circuit where ingredients and options are limited, but in the towns, particularly in Pokhara and Kathmandu, you really can get just about anything.
